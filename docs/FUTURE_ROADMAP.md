These features are NOT implemented. They are tracked here for future reference.

## Frozen Technical Enhancements

### Advanced ZK
- Recursive proofs
- Proof aggregation
- Privacy-preserving audit

### Advanced Escrow
- Milestone-based escrow
- Multi-party escrow
- Conditional release

### Advanced Reputation
- Time-weighted reputation
- Domain-specific reputation
- Reputation portability

### Advanced Permissions
- RBAC (role-based access control)
- ABAC (attribute-based access control)
- Policy engine

## Decision: Why Frozen

These features are valuable but not required for the core product:

**Core product**: "Give AI agents temporary, revocable authority to perform economic actions."

Everything above is either:
1. A future use case (DAO, insurance, collectives)
2. A technical enhancement (advanced ZK, recursive proofs)
3. A business model extension (grants, training marketplace)

**Ship the core first. Add these when customers ask for them.**
